How to Use

  1. Allow orientation access when prompted (for volume button simulation)
  2. Click the volume buttons on the phone mockup or use your actual phone's volume buttons
  3. Volume Up turns on the next light, Volume Down turns off the last light
  4. You can also use the manual buttons below
Tilt your phone forward to simulate Volume Up, backward for Volume Down
